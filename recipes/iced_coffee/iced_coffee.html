Iced coffee

Description

Drink coffee on hot days!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of instant coffee
  • 1/2 tablespoon of sugar (can add more if needed)
  • 2 tablespoons of cold water
  • ice as preferred
  • 200ml of cold milk

How to make

  1. add the instant coffee and the sugar in a glass
  2. pour the cold water into the glass
  3. stir it until foam is formed
  4. add the cold milk
  5. add the ice cubes
  6. enjoy your iced coffee!
