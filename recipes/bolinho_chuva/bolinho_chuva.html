Bolinho de chuva

Description

Bolinho de chuva is a classic Brazilian dessert. It is mildly sweet,chewy and crispy outside, try it!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of milk(240ml)
  • 1 tablespoon of yeast
  • 1 tablespoon of cinnamon powder
  • 1/2 cup of sugar(240ml)
  • 2 and 1/2 cups of wheat flour
  • oil

How to make

  1. Add two eggs and 1 cup of milk inside a bowl
  2. Mix it for about 1 minute and then add the wheat flour completely
  3. Mix until a dough is formed
  4. Add 1 tablespoon of yeast and mix it further until the dough is smooth and homogeneous
  5. Pour enough oil in a pan to fry the dough
  6. With a spoon take small parts of dough and put them in the oil
  7. Let them fry until they're browned, then put them aside on a plate
  8. Do this until you use up all the dough
  9. Prepare the coating mixing sugar and cinnamon powder
  10. Finally coat the dumplings and enjoy these delicious Bolinhos de chuva!
