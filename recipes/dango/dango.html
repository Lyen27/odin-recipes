Mitarashi Dango

Description

Originated from the Kamo Mitarashi tea house in Kyoto, mitarashi dango is a classic japanese dessert.Try it if you ever go to Japan, or make it at home!

Ingredients

  • Dough:
    • 150g of tofu
    • 100g of rice flour
  • Tare sauce:
    • 200g of sugar
    • 200ml of shoyu
    • 100ml of mirin
    • A few slices of ginger
  • Some skewers

How to make

  • Dough:
    1. Mix the tofu and rice flour inside a bowl
    2. Knead the mixture for about 5 minutes until a dough is formed
    3. Shape the dough with your hands to form a dango ball, make as much as you can
    4. Cook the dango balls in boiling water until they start to float
    5. Let the dango balls cool down in cold water for 2 minutes
    6. Skewer the dango balls
    7. Set up a pan with a bit of oil for frying the dango
    8. Fry until they get a bit browned, don't forget to flip them
    9. They're ready!, now you just have to coat the dangos in tare and enjoy
  • Tare sauce:
    1. In a pan, add all the shoyu, mirin and sugar
    2. In low fire, stir the mixture until it starts boiling
    3. Add the ginger slices and wait for the sauce to thicken
    4. Take the ginger slices out when the boiling time reaches 15 minutes
    5. Turn off the fire once the sauce has been boiling for 30 minutes
    6. Enjoy!
